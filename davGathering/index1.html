About Us

We're on a mission to bridge the gap between academic research and business innovation.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, ResearchConnect was born from a simple observation: startups struggle to access specialized research expertise, while researchers often lack opportunities to apply their knowledge to real-world problems.

Our platform bridges this gap by creating meaningful connections between these two worlds, fostering innovation and enabling groundbreaking solutions to today's challenges.

We believe that when brilliant minds collaborate across disciplines and sectors, amazing things happen. Our mission is to make these collaborations seamless and successful.
